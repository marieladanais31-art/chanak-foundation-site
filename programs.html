Programs

Chanak Academy programs are designed to strengthen educational access, student confidence, and family-school collaboration through practical nonprofit support.

Flagship Program

Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose

This flagship initiative equips students with communication, decision-making, and leadership competencies that support success in school and beyond.

Delivered with school collaboration and family alignment, the program reinforces purpose-driven development, responsibility, and constructive community participation.

Scholarships

Targeted charitable support to keep learners connected to formal education pathways.

Educational Materials

Learning packs and practical educational resources for students and households.

Family Support

Guidance sessions that strengthen family capacity to support student outcomes.

Community Learning Initiatives

Mission-aligned activities that build educational confidence and social responsibility.

School Collaboration

Program coordination with educators to ensure relevance and continuity.

Student Development Planning

Purpose-oriented mentoring that connects education with long-term goals.

Programs

Purpose-driven programs for student growth, family support, and school collaboration.

Chanak Academy programs are designed to strengthen educational access and learner success through practical, community-rooted nonprofit support.

Flagship Initiative

Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose

This flagship program helps students build confidence, character, communication, and real-world leadership habits while staying aligned with school success goals.

  • Purpose-centered leadership workshops in partnership with schools and educators
  • Life-skills development: communication, resilience, responsibility, and teamwork
  • Mentored pathways that connect student development with educational continuity
  • Family-informed approach reinforcing growth at home, in school, and in community

Scholarships

Targeted support that helps students continue formal education and overcome financial barriers.

Educational Materials

Learning resources and supplemental content that strengthen instruction and student readiness.

Family Support

Guidance and workshops that equip families to better support educational participation and continuity.

Programs

Purpose-driven programs for student growth, family support, and school collaboration.

Chanak Academy programs strengthen educational access and learner success through practical, community-rooted nonprofit support for students and families.

Flagship Initiative

Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose

This flagship program equips students with leadership habits, communication strengths, and decision-making skills that reinforce both classroom progress and long-term personal development.

  • Purpose-centered leadership workshops developed with educators and mentors
  • Life-skills coaching in communication, resilience, responsibility, and teamwork
  • Mentored learning pathways connecting student growth with school continuity
  • Family-informed support that helps learning goals remain consistent at home and in school

How the Program Supports Students

Sessions are structured to build confidence, strengthen positive peer engagement, and connect leadership practice to academic persistence and community service.

Scholarships

Targeted support helping students continue formal education and reduce financial barriers to participation.

Educational Materials

Learning resources and practical tools that reinforce instruction and improve student readiness.

Family Support

Guidance and workshops that equip families to support student attendance, motivation, and continuity.

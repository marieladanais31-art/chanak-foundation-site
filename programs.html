Programs

Chanak Academy programs are designed to strengthen educational access and learner success through practical, community-rooted support.

Scholarships

Targeted assistance to help learners continue formal education and reduce financial barriers.

Educational Materials

Learning packs, guidance materials, and supplemental content for students and families.

Family Support

Workshops and family-focused sessions that help households support students effectively.

Training Initiatives

Community learning sessions that build academic confidence and practical educational skills.

Programs

Whole-Student Programs Built for Continuity and Growth

Chanak Academy programs are designed to strengthen educational access and student success through practical, community-based nonprofit support. We focus on whole-student development, addressing both academic continuity and essential life skills.

Flagship Program

Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose

This flagship program helps students build confidence, character, communication, and real-world leadership habits while staying aligned with school success goals.

  • Purpose-centered leadership workshops
  • Life-skills development in communication, resilience, responsibility, and teamwork
  • Mentored growth pathways that reinforce student identity, purpose, and consistency
  • Family-informed support to strengthen student follow-through beyond the classroom
  • Active collaboration with schools and educators

Program Delivery Approach

Activities are delivered through practical workshops, community engagement, mentoring support, and school-aligned partnership to help students apply leadership values in daily life.

Scholarships

Chanak Academy seeks to provide scholarship support that helps qualified students and families continue educational participation when financial or contextual barriers would otherwise limit access.

Depending on available resources, scholarship support may help sustain continuity, participation, and mission-aligned educational opportunity.

Educational Materials

Chanak Academy provides educational materials and practical learning resources intended to strengthen instruction, student engagement, and learning continuity.

These resources may include learning tools, supplemental materials, and supports that help students and families participate more fully in educational growth.

Family Support

Chanak Academy offers family-centered guidance, workshops, and practical support that help caregivers engage more effectively in student success, school continuity, and personal development.

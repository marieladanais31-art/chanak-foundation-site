Mission

Building Values-Centered Support for Families, Youth, and Communities

Chanak Foundation exists to strengthen families and guide youth through practical, community-rooted educational support. We serve with accountability, collaboration, and long-term commitment so churches, caregivers, and local partners can respond to real needs with trust and consistency.

Values-Based Education Family Strengthening Youth Guidance Community Partnerships

Our Mission

Mission in Practice

To strengthen educational equity and student success through practical, community-rooted programs that integrate leadership development, family support, and strategic collaboration with schools and local institutions.

This mission is lived out through mentoring, parent engagement, youth formation pathways, and church-based partnerships that give families accessible support close to home.

Why This Work Matters

Families and Youth Are Facing Complex Social and Emotional Pressures

Across many communities, families are actively seeking educational options shaped by values, stronger relational support, and healthier developmental environments for children and adolescents.

Values and Formation

Parents are looking for education that reinforces character, responsibility, and healthy identity formation, not only academic outcomes.

Bullying and Social Pressure

Children and teens often face bullying and peer dynamics that can affect confidence, belonging, and long-term wellbeing.

Emotional Wellbeing

Families and local leaders report concern about emotional strain, anxiety, and the need for trusted spaces where youth can be heard and guided.

Technology Overexposure

Heavy device use and unhealthy digital habits can weaken attention, relationships, and healthy routines at home and in school life.

Need for Mentoring

Many youth need consistent mentors who provide orientation, accountability, and practical encouragement through key life stages.

Personalized Support

Standardized environments do not meet every learner's needs; families value closer, relational, and individualized accompaniment.

Local Partnerships in Action

Working in Collaboration with Local Partners

Chanak Foundation works in collaboration with local partners such as EducaFe in the development of community-rooted projects in churches across Tarragona, Jávea, Vinaròs, and Torrevieja. These projects are designed to offer families meaningful options shaped by education in values, family strengthening, youth support, and practical care for today's challenges.

In this collaboration context, initiatives respond to concerns raised by families and local leaders, including emotional wellbeing among children and adolescents, bullying and social pressure, unhealthy technology habits, and the need for stronger moral and relational guidance.

Program development emphasizes mentoring, family accompaniment, and church-based environments where support can be personalized, trustworthy, and close to the daily realities of each community.

Local Collaboration Partner

EducaFe is presented as a local implementation partner within Chanak Foundation's broader mission-led framework.

Our Vision

A Future of Healthy Formation and Shared Responsibility

A future in which every student, regardless of background, has access to educational support that develops leadership, resilience, and purpose through strong family and community partnership.

We envision churches, families, educators, and local organizations working together so children and youth can grow with clear values, practical direction, and stable support relationships.

Core Values

Principles That Guide Every Program and Partnership

Empowerment

We help students and families build confidence, agency, and practical tools for educational and relational growth.

Collaboration

We work with caregivers, churches, educators, and local partners to deliver coordinated and trustworthy support.

Equity

We prioritize fair access to educational opportunity and support for families facing barriers.

Integrity

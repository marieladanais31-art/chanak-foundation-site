Mission

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Chanak Foundation advances whole-person educational support through accountable nonprofit service and community partnership.

Mission

To strengthen educational equity and student success through practical, community-rooted programs that integrate leadership development, family support, and strategic collaboration with schools.

Vision

A future in which every student, regardless of background, has access to educational support that develops leadership, resilience, and purpose through strong family and community partnership.

Core Values

Empowerment

We help students and families build confidence, agency, and practical tools for educational growth.

Collaboration

We work with caregivers, educators, and community partners to deliver coordinated student support.

Equity

We prioritize fair access to educational opportunities and support for students facing barriers.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and commitment to the public benefit mission of CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC.

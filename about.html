About Chanak Foundation

Chanak Foundation exists in response to the growing need for more holistic and accessible educational support.

Our Purpose

As an initiative of CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC., Chanak Foundation works alongside families, educators, and community partners to help students grow academically, emotionally, and relationally.

Our nonprofit approach is built on practical support, consistent partnership, and a long-term commitment to educational continuity for students and families.

What Makes Our Approach Distinct

  • Whole-person focus that includes academic, social, and leadership development
  • Family-informed programming that respects the role of caregivers in student success
  • Collaboration with schools and educators to align support with real needs
  • Commitment to transparent, mission-driven nonprofit service

About Chanak Academy

Chanak Academy is a nonprofit educational initiative operated by CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC. We are dedicated to expanding educational opportunity through scholarships, practical learning support, and family-centered guidance.

Who We Are

Our work is mission-led and community-informed. We collaborate with educators, volunteers, and local partners to strengthen educational continuity and learner success.

We focus on access, engagement, and long-term growth by combining student support services with family partnership and school collaboration.

On the Ground

Meet the Families Behind the Mission

These are not employees. These are families who left comfort behind to build something that didn't exist.

TG

Torrevieja

Mary Claudia García Jiménez & Henry Granda

Mary Claudia and Henry moved their family to Torrevieja with one conviction: Christian families on Spain's Costa Blanca deserved a real educational alternative. They are building it from scratch, one family at a time.

JR

Jávea

Medalith Rosales & Juan Carlos González

Medalith and Juan Carlos saw the gap between what public schools offered and what their faith required. Today they are founding the Jávea Hub — the first faith-integrated learning community on the northern Costa Blanca.

VA

Vinarós

Dayana Trillo & Mario Artis

Dayana and Mario are pioneering Christian education in Vinarós, a city where no evangelical educational alternative existed. Their Hub is a lighthouse for families who felt alone in their convictions.

TU

Tarragona

Thiare Urzua & Sergio Masía

Thiare and Sergio are laying the groundwork for Chanak's presence in Catalonia — working with local churches and Christian families who are ready to build something lasting for their children.

Spain is the 5th largest economy in the EU. Yet for the less than 2% of the population who identify as evangelical Christian, faith-based education is effectively inaccessible. Chanak and EducaFe exist to change that — one Hub, one family, one child at a time.

Privacy and Data Use

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026. This Privacy Policy describes how CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC. (operating through Chanak Foundation) may collect, use, and share information when you visit this website or contact us.

Who We Are

Chanak Foundation is an educational initiative of CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC., a U.S. nonprofit organization. Mailing address: 7901 4th St N, Ste 300, St. Petersburg, FL 33702. Email: administration@chanakacademy.org.

Information We Collect

  • Contact form details you submit, such as your name, email address, organization, topic, and message content.
  • Donation-related information you choose to provide through donation links or donation inquiries (for example, contact details and donation intent). Payment processing data is handled by the external payment platform used at checkout.
  • Communications information when you email us directly.
  • Basic technical and usage data that may be collected automatically by website infrastructure, cookies, or analytics tools in use.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries from churches, donors, partners, schools, and families.
  • To provide organizational updates and administrative communication relevant to your request.
  • To improve website content, user experience, and nonprofit operations.
  • To support stewardship, internal reporting, and mission-aligned administration.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for essential site functions and website analytics. We use this information in aggregate where possible to understand site usage and improve communications. If analytics tools are changed or expanded, this section should be reviewed and updated.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that support website operation, communications, or payment processing, and when required by law or to protect legal rights. Data sharing is limited to legitimate nonprofit operational needs.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for communication, donor relations, record-keeping, legal obligations, and nonprofit administration. Retention periods may vary by the nature of the request or transaction.

Your Rights and Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have submitted to us, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements. To make a privacy request, contact administration@chanakacademy.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or technical changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact Information

Privacy questions can be sent to administration@chanakacademy.org or mailed to CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC., 7901 4th St N, Ste 300, St. Petersburg, FL 33702, United States.

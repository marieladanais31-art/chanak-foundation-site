Who We Are

Community-Rooted, Mission-Driven Educational Support

Chanak Academy is an educational initiative of CHANAK TRAINUP EDUCATION, INC., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to strengthening educational access, leadership development, and family-centered support.

We believe education extends beyond academics and should equip students with character, resilience, and practical life skills that sustain long-term growth.

Impact

Chanak Academy works toward meaningful impact by helping students remain connected to educational pathways, strengthening family engagement, and reinforcing leadership development that supports long-term success.

Our nonprofit approach emphasizes continuity, trusted partnership with schools, and practical community-informed support that grows over time.

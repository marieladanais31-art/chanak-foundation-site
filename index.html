Who We Are

Chanak Academy is an educational initiative focused on learners and families who benefit from structured support, practical guidance, and community-rooted programs. We collaborate with educators, volunteers, and local partners to create pathways that improve educational outcomes and long-term opportunity.

Featured Program: Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose

Our Leadership and Life Skills with Purpose program equips young people with communication, problem-solving, teamwork, confidence, and ethical decision-making skills that support both school success and responsible citizenship.

Through guided sessions, mentoring, and project-based learning, participants build practical habits for personal growth, community contribution, and long-term educational resilience.

What We Do

Scholarship Support

We provide scholarship opportunities that help students continue their studies and access learning tools that might otherwise be out of reach.

Educational Resources

We supply curriculum-aligned materials, mentoring support, and practical learning resources for students and families.

Family & Community Initiatives

We offer workshops and support services that strengthen family participation in education and encourage community-based learning.

Impact Approach

Student-Centered Outcomes

Programs are designed to improve participation, confidence, and educational progression through practical and measurable support.

Family Engagement

Families receive guidance and tools that help reinforce student learning and improve home-school collaboration.

Community Partnerships

We partner with local stakeholders to keep initiatives relevant, accessible, and responsive to community educational needs.
